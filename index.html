About Our School

Sunrise Academy - Where Dreams Take Flight

Established in 1995, Sunrise Academy has been a beacon of quality education for over 25 years. We are committed to providing holistic education that develops not just academic excellence but also character, creativity, and critical thinking.

Our experienced faculty uses innovative teaching methods to ensure every student reaches their full potential. We believe in creating an environment where learning is joyful and meaningful.

Our Mission: To provide quality education and shape future leaders with strong moral values.
